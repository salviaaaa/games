Tic Tac Toe

How to Play Tic Tac Toe

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Two players take turns placing X and O marks in empty squares.
  • The first player uses symbol X, and the second player uses symbol O.
  • The winner is the player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row.
  • If all squares are filled and no player has won, the game ends in a draw.
