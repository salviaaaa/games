Checkers

How to Play Checkers

  • The game is played on an 8x8 board of black and white squares.
  • Each player starts with 12 pieces on their side of the board.
  • Pieces can only move diagonally forward on black squares.
  • Pieces can jump over opponent's pieces if there's an empty space behind them.
  • When a piece reaches the furthest row, it becomes a "King" and can move forward or backward.
  • The game ends when one player loses all their pieces.
