Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

  • In Flappy Bird, your task is to keep the bird flying and avoid obstacles.
  • Click or press space to make the bird fly higher.
  • Avoid the green pipes that appear as obstacles.
  • Every time you pass through a pair of pipes, you earn a point.
  • The game ends if the bird hits a pipe or falls to the ground.
