Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

  • Click with your mouse or press the Spacebar key to make the bird fly upward
  • Each click or press makes the bird flap its wings and gain height
  • Navigate through the pipes without hitting them
  • Each pipe you pass earns you 1 point
  • The game ends if the bird hits a pipe or the ground
